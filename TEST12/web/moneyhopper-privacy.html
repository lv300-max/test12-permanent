MoneyHopper Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-07

Overview

MoneyHopper is a lightweight shift timer and earnings visualizer. It is designed to work offline and store your progress on-device. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data We Collect

  • Local app state stored on your device (rate, shift progress, and saved totals).
  • Purchase status information from Google Play (only if you purchase or restore optional items).

MoneyHopper does not require an account and does not run its own backend for user data.

How We Use Data

  • Compute and display your earnings and progress.
  • Save your totals locally so your progress persists between sessions.
  • Restore optional purchases (when available) through Google Play.
