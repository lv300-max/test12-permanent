Test~12 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

Overview

Test~12 verifies app submissions, checks Google Play install/open status, and tracks limited diagnostic signals to keep the queue fair. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can reach us.

Data We Collect

  • App submission details: app name, Play Store link, and display name.
  • Phone number you provide for verification and cycle locking.
  • Device/app signals tied to your submission: install/open status, check-in timestamps, and verification outcomes.
  • Basic app diagnostics (crash and performance data) to keep the service stable.

How We Use Data

  • Verify your app against Google Play and confirm assigned installs/opens.
  • Enforce one active submission per phone number and maintain fair rotation.
  • Send status updates and notifications you opt into.
  • Improve stability and detect fraud or misuse.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only what is needed with service providers that support verification (for example, Google Play checks) under confidentiality obligations or when required by law.

Retention

Submission and verification data are retained for the active cycle and then purged when operational needs end, unless a longer period is required for legal or security reasons.

Your Choices

  • Disable notifications at any time in-app or via your device settings.
  • Request removal of your submission data; we will delete it unless retention is required by law.

Security

We use access controls, encryption in transit, and operational safeguards to protect the data we process. No system is perfect, so please contact us if you suspect an issue.
